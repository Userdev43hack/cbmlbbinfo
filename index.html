Gameplay Mechanics Tips

Bush Trick

When entering a bush, an eye appears on the health bar. If the eye appears slowly, an enemy is nearby in other bushes. If it appears quickly, there are no nearby enemies. This helps you quickly analyze enemy presence without risking entering other bushes. This is a very neat trick for lower ranks, but higher ranks generally know this.

Early Game Jungle Assistance

If you're a mage, help your ally jungler at the start of the game to speed up their jungling. This benefits the jungler significantly. Then, consider visiting your gold lane teammate to increase their chances of securing an early kill.

Roaming Support

When roaming, prioritize helping your ally jungler (similar to the mage's role). Roaming in the gold lane and mid lane is often more impactful than focusing solely on experience as an EXP laner. A strong mindset for the EXP laner is important.
