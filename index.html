Gameplay Mechanics Tips

Bush Trick

When entering a bush, an eye appears on the health bar. If the eye appears slowly, an enemy is nearby. If it appears quickly, no enemies are nearby. This helps analyze enemy presence without risking entering other bushes.

Early Game Jungle Assistance

If you're a mage, help your ally jungler at the start to speed up their jungling. This benefits the jungler significantly. Then, consider visiting your gold lane teammate.

Roaming Support

When roaming, prioritize helping your ally jungler. Roaming in the gold and mid lanes is often more impactful than focusing solely on experience as an EXP laner.
